Life mission statement

Grow as an independent being and help people I care about grow. My memory will disappear soon enough after my last breath, my money won't serve me anymore, my stuff won't last forever but if I did exist for those few people I'll be done here. I might not change the world but I do hope I'll change their world.

Values

Be brutally honest

  • To me, I'll be courageous enough to live a life true to myself, not the life others expected of me
  • To others, I'll strive for an open communication by listening, understanding only then responding with honesty
  • In action, I'll act my words and live my acts
